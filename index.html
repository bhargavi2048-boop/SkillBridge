SkillBridge combines resume parsing, AI gap analysis, visual skill mapping, and peer-to-peer skill exchange — all in one platform built for Indian students.

🎯
Domain-Locked AI Analysis
Choose your career domain — Graphic Design, Cybersecurity, Law, Medicine — and every insight, skill gap, roadmap, and certification is laser-focused on that domain. No more generic tech advice when you're targeting a creative role.
16 DomainsDomain-Specific AIClaude API
⇄
Peer Skill Exchange
Trade your strengths for skills you need. SkillSwap's AI matching engine finds students whose teaching aligns perfectly with your learning gaps — zero cost, zero friction.
AI Matching50+ Campuses

Interactive Radar Chart
A canvas-rendered hexagonal radar shows your skill profile vs. job requirements — domain-specific axes, not generic bars. See exactly where you stand.
Canvas APIDomain Axes

30-Day AI Roadmap
Personalized week-by-week plan with resources, projects, and tasks tailored to close your specific gaps in your specific domain.
Week-by-WeekDomain-Specific

PDF Resume Parser
Upload your resume in PDF format. Instantly extracts skills, experience, education, and projects using PDF.js + Claude AI. Or paste text directly.
